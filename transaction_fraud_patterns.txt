TRANSACTION-RELATED FRAUD PATTERNS

TRANSACTION FRAUD PATTERNS:
- Multiple small transactions testing card limits
- Large purchases at unusual merchants for account holder
- Rapid-fire transactions at same merchant
- Transactions in geographic locations far from cardholder's home
- Structured deposits/withdrawals to avoid reporting thresholds
- Circular transactions between related accounts
- Third-party payments without clear business relationship

MONEY LAUNDERING PATTERNS:
- Structured cash deposits just below reporting thresholds
- Rapid movement of funds between multiple accounts
- Unusual business account activity inconsistent with stated purpose
- Layering through complex transaction chains
- Integration into legitimate business accounts

CHECK FRAUD PATTERNS:
- Altered payee names or amounts on legitimate checks
- Counterfeit checks with valid account information
- Check kiting between institutions
- Forged endorsements
- Duplicate check presentment
