DIGITAL AND CARD FRAUD PATTERNS

PHISHING AND SOCIAL ENGINEERING:
- Impersonation of bank officials to obtain credentials
- Fake emergency scenarios to bypass security
- Authority impersonation to manipulate employees
- Vishing attacks targeting elderly customers
- Business email compromise targeting corporate accounts

ATM AND CARD FRAUD:
- Skimming devices installed on card readers
- Shoulder surfing to capture PINs
- Card trapping mechanisms retaining cards
- Cash trapping in dispenser mechanisms
- False front panels on ATM interfaces

ONLINE BANKING FRAUD:
- Man-in-the-middle attacks intercepting transactions
- Malware capturing keystrokes and credentials
- Fake mobile banking applications
- Credential stuffing attacks using breached data
- DNS spoofing redirecting to fake banking sites

CREDIT CARD FRAUD:
- Account number generation algorithms testing valid numbers
- Merchant collusion schemes
- Friendly fraud chargebacks
- Card-not-present fraud with stolen details
- Account stacking with multiple authorized users
